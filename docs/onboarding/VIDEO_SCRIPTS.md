## Production Notes

- Use **Loom** or **OBS** for screen recording at 1920×1080
- Export final cut as `H.264 MP4` at `3–5 Mbps` bitrate
- Keep files **under 10 MB** (compress with HandBrake if needed)
- Export poster frame as `JPEG @ 80% quality` — aim for < 150 KB
- Record voiceover after screen capture to match exactly
- Use a **lapel mic** or quality USB mic — clear audio matters more than perfect video
- Add **subtle zoom animations** to highlight UI elements (built into Loom Pro)
- No background music in first 3 s (videos autoplay muted — silence = better UX)
